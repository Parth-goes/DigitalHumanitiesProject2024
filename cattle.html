Common Myna

Acridotheres tristis

Family: Sturnidae Brood parasite Omnivorous

The Asian koel is a member of the cuckoo family found in the Indian subcontinent, China, and Southeast Asia. It forms a superspecies with the closely related black-billed and Pacific koels, which are sometimes treated as subspecies.

RangeIndian subcontinent, China, SE Asia
Diet (adult)Largely frugivorous
Name origin The name koel is echoic in origin with several language variants.
Cultural roleIt is a symbol in Indian, Pakistani & Nepali poetry
